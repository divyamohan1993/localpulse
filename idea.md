LocalPulse" AI Crisis Management for Small Communities
The Problem: During local emergencies (floods, fires, or even major power outages), information on social media is scattered and often full of rumors. Small towns don't have the budget for high-end "smart city" software.
The Practical Solution: A lightweight, mobile-first dashboard for local residents and emergency responders.
The AI Part:
Social Media Summarization: An AI agent that scrapes local Twitter/X or Reddit threads and uses NLP to filter out noise, providing a "Status Summary" of blocked roads or open shelters.
Multilingual Voice Bot: An AI phone line (using Twilio + Whisper) where elderly residents can call in to report issues or get updates in their native language without needing a smartphone.
